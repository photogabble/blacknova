а Strategy - Banker
а How to use interest rates to become rich.

 
The banker focuses on building interest.

Upgrade your planet as if you were a builder. Be sure that the Planet is completely well defended. Consistently spend 10% of your turns adding Fighters and torpedoes to the Planet. Place all of your money on your planet, and land on the planet. It should be well defended enough to survive any attacks.

Wait 2 hours, during which the money will earn interest. Then play 600 turns as though you were a Trader. At the end, put the new money on the Planet and wait another 2 hours before you play again.

The important thing is to let the money sit around and accrue interest for as long as possible.

This strategy works fairly well if you combine it with a Builder, although it is harder to defend your empire this way.

Banking allows you to earn a very competitive amount of credits when compared with builders, but you can't actively play as often, and losing a single planet can be devastating. Banking is not nearly as effective as it once was. It is now fairly similar in efficiency to the builder strategy.
