  Overview
  A general look at Nova Gaming System.

 Nova Gaming System began as a simple web-based trading game. As time goes on, we are getting closer to our goal of a truly modular system upon which other game genres can be built.

The initial game "The Kabal Invasion" is due to be released near the end of 2002. The initial release will not be truly modular, but will be an attempt to finalize the game play and style before the move to a modular system.

What you are playing today is an early beta of that final product. A stepping stone, if you will, towards our eventual goal. As such, there will be things that are not ideal yet. There are going to be rough edges, bugs, and exploits.

Only through dedicated testing and playing can we hope to tackle those issues. We hope that you will provide us with bug reports, so that future versions of the game will be more of what you want, and less of what you don't.

Please read through the other sections, located on the left hand side of this page, to understand the game better. When you have learned enough, the back2play! button will take you back to the main menu of the game.

Enjoy!
